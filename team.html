Why is everyone picking LEOT

We do not think of ourselves as just another tutorial center. We believe that learning does not have to be tedious. By providing students with a warm and accepting environment, we make sure that every student feels welcomed and feels encouraged to learn. We strive to make LEOT as afforable as possible while still providing the best education possible. Every question will appear like a puzzle, and the student will be provided all the secrets in solving it. We know that we WILL make a difference, which is why the first session is FREE! If you love us, you can enroll. If not, you never need to come back again, but we really think you’ll love us.

Students are only as good as their tutor

We truly believe that a student’s education is only as good as a tutor’s teaching method. We have a rigorous hiring system to ensure that we are only taking the best of the best. We have tutors from all around the city, ranging from Specialized High-School alumnies to tutors granted full scholarships to the colleges of their dreams. By only taking the hardest working tutors, you can ensure that you are receiving the best education by the best possible teachers.

Our Programs

NY State Test

Each of our tutors are exceptional with the Common Core curriculum for both ELA and Math. With our 2-hour classes, you can ensure that your child will improve with each and every session, to help achieve that 4!

SHSAT Class

Each of our tutors are specialized high-school alumnies and have taught and adapted to the new SHSAT. In a small classroom setting, the student will learn everything needed for the SHSAT, as well as receive the individual attention deserved!

Regents

The student will be paired with a tutor that has done exceptionally well on the Regents exam that the student needs help with. This way, the tutor can help teach tips and tricks that the tutor themselves have learned along the way.
