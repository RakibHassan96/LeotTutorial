Regents

Regents are HARD! That’s the truth… But our tutors have excelled on them. To be hired for that specific subject, the tutor has to provide proof that he or she received at least a 90 on the exam. After that, we give them a test and a tutoring practice session to see if they really can teach the subject. We think this is the only way to make sure that we are not wasting the student’s time. Trust us when we say, we will make the regents EASY for you!
